Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our security services

How fast can your security team be on site?

We can deploy teams within 2–4 hours for local assignments. For high-risk locations or specialized requests, we plan logistics carefully to ensure safety and compliance with PSIRA standards.

What screening process do your personnel undergo?

All Blackout VIP personnel undergo a rigorous screening process including comprehensive background checks, psychological evaluations, skills assessment, and continuous performance monitoring.

Do you offer armed patrols or armored vehicle services?

Yes. Our armed mobile patrols and armored vehicles provide secure, rapid-response protection across South Africa. Teams are trained, fully equipped, and ready to safeguard your property, assets, or personnel.

How do you ensure client confidentiality?

We follow strict confidentiality protocols with non-disclosure agreements, secure communications, and compartmentalized operations. Discretion is built into every aspect of our service.

Why choose Blackout VIP over other security companies?

Our team combines elite, PSIRA-certified personnel, advanced security technology, and proactive risk assessment strategies. With a focus on reliability, discretion, and local expertise, we deliver a level of service that stands out in South Africa.
