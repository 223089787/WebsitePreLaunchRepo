Our Story

Blackout VIP was founded with a singular mission — to redefine security excellence through precision, discretion, and integrity.

Blackout VIP emerged from a recognized need for truly elite security services that combine military precision with corporate discretion. Our founders, with backgrounds in special operations and executive protection, saw a gap in the market for security that doesn't just react to threats, but anticipates and neutralizes them before they materialize.

Over the years, we've grown from a small team of specialists to an established security firm, yet we've maintained our core philosophy: every client deserves personalized, proactive protection.
